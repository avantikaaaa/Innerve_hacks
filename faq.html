FAQs

Who can become a Campus Ambassador? +

You only need to be a college student in order to become a part of the Campus Ambassador Program. All you need is a valid college ID and you are good to go.

What are the criteria on which a Campus Ambassador is judged? +

You can Contact our Team by "Contact Us" section available on the CA website. Our Team will get back to you through the Email Address or Phone Number provided by you

How to become a Campus Ambassador? +

In order to become a Campus Ambassador you need to visit codingninjas.com and register yourself by providing the required details.

How can a CA get assistance from Coding Ninja when required? +

You can Contact our Team by "Contact Us" section available on the CA website. Our Team will get back to you through the Email Address or Phone Number provided by you.

What are the responsibilities as a CA? +

A CA has to advertise, promote, coordinate, conduct and organize various events in their respective college.
